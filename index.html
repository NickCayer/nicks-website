Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Do you offer discounts for multiple services? +
Yes. When you bundle window cleaning with pressure washing or solar panel cleaning, you'll save compared to booking each separately.
How often should I have my windows cleaned? +
Every 3-4 months for exteriors, once or twice a year for interiors. Homes near busy roads may need more frequent service.
How long does a typical job take? +
Most window cleaning jobs take 2-4 hours. Pressure washing runs 1-3 hours depending on the area. We'll give you a time estimate with your quote.
Do I need to be home during the service? +
Not for exterior work. Many clients leave a garage code or key. For interior cleaning, we'll need access inside.
Will pressure washing damage my property? +
No. We use surface-appropriate pressure and chemical-free cleaning when possible. Every surface gets exactly the right treatment—never more than it can handle.
